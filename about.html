About Me

Pastor Temaka L. Ford is a resident of Summerville, SC she’s the mother of two wonderful children; Dontrel Eadie and Madison Ford; she’s also the wife of 11 years to the awesome Pastor Sunil Ford.

After many years of dismissing prophecy spoken to her of being a Pastor, in April 2015 Temaka L. Ford became Senior Pastor and Founder of Restoring Life Worship Center. Born in Charleston, South Carolina on May 21, 1988, and raised in Summerville, South Carolina, Temaka L. Ford is a May 2004 high school graduate of Vision Christian Academy in Summerville South Carolina and a December 2009 graduate of Centura College in North Charleston South Carolina as a Licensed Practical Nurse. This enabled her to work with well-known doctors and major companies in the Charleston areas.

An advocate of higher promotion; Pastor Temaka L. Ford became the author of "The Beauty of A Rebellious Child." This 2014 publication is a story of her life, based on true-life events. The book is available nationwide on some of the highest platforms. Barnes and Noble was one of the first outlets that wanted copies of this publication. The Beauty of a Rebellious Child is now in Statewide county jails and prisons. Pastor Temaka L. Ford didn’t stop there in 2018 she published her sophomore book entitled “Live Over It” which has given Pastor Temaka L. Ford opportunities to appear on national television shows such as Preach The Word Network and many others. The book's success has also opened doors for mentoring & counseling opportunities. Pastor Temaka L. Ford has recently released her first EP single March of 2021 entitled “Secret Place”. The single is available on all digital outlets. She is also the founder and CEO of LadyLike By Temaka clothing line and Mek Treats Bakery.

Temaka L. Ford's spiritual journey began at The House of God Keith Dominion (holiness) Church in St. Stephens, South Carolina. There she was promoted from being a choir member to head choir director locally and nationally. In 2010 Temaka L. Ford followed God's leading to serve as administrator for her brother, Apostle Sharell L. Eadie, at the non-denominational church, Totally His Ministries International.

Continuing to grow in ministry and hearing God's voice, Temaka L. Ford gave God a True Yes to His will and became a minister in 2011. For two years she faithfully preached and taught God's Word. Remaining humble Pastor Temaka L. Ford answered a greater and deeper calling to become an ordain Elder in August 2013, enabling her to be in charge of church services in the pastor's absence. With release from her leader she was release to birth her ministry. Pastor Temaka L. Ford is the founder and visionary of Restoring Life Worship Center located in Summerville South Carolina. God has been assigning people to this ministry for the past 6 years.
